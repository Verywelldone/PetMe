All About Me

Dedicated to my passion and adoration for animal care, I became a professional Dog Sitter in 2000. Ever since, I have been providing loyal and loving pet care and services to the San Francisco area and beyond.

I pride myself on my patience, kindness and competence — and my clients would agree. It’s why so many of them use my services on a regular basis. Remember, no job is too big or too small for me to handle, so please get in touch. I can’t wait to start looking after your precious pet.
